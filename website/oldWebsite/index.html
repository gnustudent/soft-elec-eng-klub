Soldering of electronics & reusing parts

Ami Ahmad Touseef will demonstrate and teach you to solder together circuit elements on perf-boards. You will unsolder with Ami Ahmad Touseef key components off useless PCBs and reuse them in building fully operational circuits, done live.

Microcontroller starter guide & tutorial

Microcontroller programming, compiliation, debugging

Michael Lescisin

Chairman

Mike gives mentorship to students on popular hardware boards, such as Arduino and Raspberry Pi. Mike also manages SEEK projects using Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and other Atmel Based Micro-controllers.

Ahmad Touseef

Vice-chairman

"Ami" Ahmad Touseef co-founded SEEK with Mike. He oversees mentoring younger peers, acquiring club funding, initiating key projects, expanding SEEK's resources and scope of reach. Ami finds peace and leisure in painting and coding.

Dhimiter Qendri

VP Embedded Systems

Dhimiter focuses on embedded related technologies such as micrcontrollers, embedded Linux ,FPGA's and PCB design.

Sagar Desai

VP Programming

Sagar focuses on software programming related projects. He is proficient at java but also knows C++ and Python. Sagar loves playing video games and coding in his spare time.

Qusay Mahmoud, PhD

Faculty Advisor

Dr. Mahmoud is passionate about research at intersections of hardware & software. He was the first to set SEEK's club mandate: to make equipment & resources available at all times to students for mastery of hardware & software.
