Welcome to Software & Electrical Eng. Klub at DC & UOIT

We give you access to student run open hardware library similar to EE Work bench. It has high end oscilloscopes, solder stations, software tools, and many EE prototyping parts, tools & boards.

We share office with McNaughton Center, at ACE 4030. Calendar shows you events and has clickable links to download presentations. As beginner you will learn to build electronics and software. Use past projects as inspiration or start your own.

We provide you with all parts you need. Any part SEEK doesn't carry, McNaughton Center's library will carry. Order parts that are not carried at SEEK or McNaughton Center with this form.

We mentor you to go from beginner to pro. Everything is free to use. Loan out parts with this form. You have freedom as pro or beginner to make and hack electronics and software. This way you make real EE & SE products.
