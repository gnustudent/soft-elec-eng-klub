Welcome to Software & Electrical Eng. Klub at DC & UOIT

We give you access to student run open hardware library similar to EE Work bench. It has high end oscilloscopes, solder stations, software tools, and many EE prototyping as current inventory of parts, tools & boards. Over September more equipment will be added as shown in future inventory.

We share office with McNaughton Center, at ACE 4020. Calendar shows you events and has clickable links to download presentations. As beginner you will learn to build electronics and software. Use past projects as inspiration or start your own.

We provide you with all parts you need. Any part SEEK doesn't carry, McNaughton Center's current inventory of parts library should carry. In case they do not, you can order parts at SEEK or McNaughton Center with this request order form.

We mentor you to go from beginner to pro. Everything is free to use. Loan out parts with this sign out form. You have freedom as pro or beginner to make and hack electronics and software. This way you make real EE & SE products.
